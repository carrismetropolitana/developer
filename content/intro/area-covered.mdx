## We're Carris _Metropolitana_

**Carris Metropolitana** and **Carris (municipal)** are two separate entities.

Carris (municipal) operates exclusively within Lisbon city boundaries, while Carris Metropolitana operates in 15 of the 18 municipalities of the Lisbon metropolitan area.
Both the GTFS and the API described in this website provide scheduled and realtime data for Carris Metropolitana.
